Privacy Policy

✓ Zero Personal Data Collected

Effective Date: 15 March 2026  |  App: Chandamama Bedtime Stories  |  Governed by: Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)

1. About This App

Chandamama ("we", "our", "the App") is a free, offline-first bedtime story application for children aged 2–10 and their parents. It runs entirely in your browser. No server, no account, no data collection.

2. Data We Collect — None (Personal)

We collect zero personal data. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, device identifiers, or any information that identifies you or your child.

3. Browser Storage (localStorage)

We store the following on your device only, in your browser's localStorage:

  • cm_consent_v1 — A timestamp of your parental consent and the birth year range you confirmed (not the exact year). Used to avoid re-showing the consent gate on return visits.
  • cm_progress — Which chapters you have read, per story. Used to resume where you left off.
  • cm_prefs — Font size preference for Reading Mode.

This data is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to any server. You can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

4. Google Fonts

This App loads two fonts (Philosopher, Nunito) from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This is the only outgoing network request. Google may log this request in accordance with their own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy. We have no control over Google's logging practices.

5. Microphone Access (Optional)

Listening Mode optionally requests access to your device's microphone. This is used solely for real-time voice activity detection — to pause narration when your child speaks. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio. The microphone stream is processed in-memory and discarded immediately. You may deny microphone access and Listening Mode will still function (without voice detection).

6. Children's Privacy (DPDPA 2023)

Under Section 9 of India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, processing personal data of children (under 18) requires verifiable parental consent. Since we collect no personal data of children, this section is satisfied. The parental consent gate is provided as a best-practice measure to ensure an adult is aware of the App's use.

7. Data Transfers

No personal data is transferred to any person, entity, or country. The App functions entirely offline except for the Google Fonts request described in Section 4.

8. Your Rights Under DPDPA 2023

  • Right to access information about data processed — we collect none, so there is nothing to access.
  • Right to correction and erasure — clear your browser's site data to erase all localStorage data.
  • Right to withdraw consent — close the browser tab. Clear localStorage to reset the consent flag.
  • Right to grievance redressal — see Section 10 below.

9. Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach. All local storage data is protected by your device and browser's security model.

10. Grievance Officer

As required by the DPDPA 2023, you may contact our Grievance Officer for any privacy-related concerns:

Name: Chandamama Grievance Officer
Email: chandamama.grievance@protonmail.com
Response Time: Within 30 days of receipt of grievance.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the Effective Date and, where applicable, show a revised consent notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
